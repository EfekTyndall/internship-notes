# DCS

## Cartesian Speed Check

- Limit 100 mm/s
- No stop
- Override limit 25%

## Safe I/o connect

- **If SAFE:** PSI[1]= OFF AND PSI[2]= OFF ---> PSO[1] = ON ---> CSC[2] = DISABLED
- **If NOT SAFE:** PSI[1]= ON AND PSI[2]= ON ---> PSO[1] = OFF ---> CSC[2] = ENABLED
